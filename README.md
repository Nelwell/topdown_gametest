# Verdant Legend

A browser-based top-down action adventure inspired by classic 16-bit adventures. Explore a wide overworld with distinct regions, delve into handcrafted dungeons, topple mighty bosses, and save your progress at arcane shrines.

## Features

- **Expansive overworld** with forest, highland, desert, swamp, and snowy biomes.
- **Three themed dungeons** capped with bespoke boss encounters.
- **Responsive combat** with a generous sword reach and dash movement.
- **Save points** that preserve your progress between sessions (via `localStorage`).
- **Pixel-art aesthetic** evoking the feel of classic SNES adventures.

## How to Play

1. Open `index.html` in a modern desktop browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, or Safari).
2. Controls:
   - Move: Arrow Keys or WASD
   - Attack: Space or **J**
   - Interact / Save: Enter or **K**
   - Dash: Shift
3. Explore the overworld, find shrines to save, and conquer each temple’s guardian.

Tip: Save before challenging a boss so you can quickly resume if you fall!
